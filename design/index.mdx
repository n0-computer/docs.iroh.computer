This section describes how iroh works at a conceptual level. It is intended for power users
and integrators who want to understand iroh's design and behavior without reading the source
code. Where the [How it works](/concepts/endpoints) pages give a high-level introduction,
these pages document the mechanisms in detail.

For the low-level wire protocol specification, see the
[wire spec](https://github.com/n0-computer/iroh/tree/main/specs/wire) in the iroh repository.

<Info>
This material is maintained as the iroh design specification (currently **v1.2**) in the
[iroh repository](https://github.com/n0-computer/iroh/tree/main/specs/design).
</Info>
