### Hello fren,

The first time you sign up for an account on Iroh Services, you are asked to create a
**project**.  A project is your control & monitoring center for your network.
You can provision relays, monitor metrics, create alerts, run simulations, and
manage workflows. 

## Get Started

- [Quickstart](/iroh-services/quickstart): Learn how to build your first Iroh Services-enabled application.
- [Access](/iroh-services/access): Understand how to manage access control for your Iroh Services project.
- [Metrics](/iroh-services/metrics): Learn about the metrics collected by Iroh Services and how to use them to monitor your network.
- [Relays](/iroh-services/relays): Discover how to provision and manage relay servers for your network.
